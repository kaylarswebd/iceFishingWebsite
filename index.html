Hello, all and welcome to the For the Love of Fishing Website!

This is a simple web site for Minnesotans to come and see tips and tricks for the current season in Minnesota. This website is mainly opion bases, with that being said the site is open for comments and suggestions! You can e-mail them to kylelarsonwebd@gmail.com
